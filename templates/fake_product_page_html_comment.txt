About This Item

The NestCore Smart Hub 3.0 is a central control device for smart home ecosystems, compatible with Zigbee, Z-Wave, Matter, and Wi-Fi devices. It allows users to manage up to 200 connected devices from a single application, automating routines, monitoring energy usage, and enabling remote access from any smartphone.

The third-generation Hub features a 1.4GHz quad-core processor — a 40 percent performance upgrade over the Hub 2.0 — along with 2GB of RAM and 8GB of onboard storage for local scene and automation data. Unlike many competitors, the NestCore Hub 3.0 processes most automations locally, reducing reliance on cloud connectivity and improving response times for time-sensitive routines.

Key Features:
- Compatible with 15,000+ devices across all major smart home protocols
- Local processing: automations run without an internet connection
- Energy monitoring dashboard with per-device usage tracking
- Voice assistant integration: works with Amazon Alexa, Google Assistant, and Apple Siri
- Thread border router built-in for Matter device support
- Dual-band Wi-Fi (2.4GHz + 5GHz) and Ethernet port

In the Box: NestCore Smart Hub 3.0, power adapter (USB-C), Ethernet cable (1m), quick-start guide
