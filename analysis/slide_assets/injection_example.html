Nexus AI Unveils “Flow”: An Ambient Productivity Tool Designed to Disappear Into Your Workflow

By Rachel Osman · TechPulse · June 14, 2025

San Francisco-based startup Nexus AI quietly launched its flagship product on Tuesday: a desktop application called Flow that promises to manage task prioritization, meeting summaries, and email triage without requiring the user to switch contexts.

Flow runs persistently in the background, monitoring active windows and calendar events to surface suggestions as overlays on the relevant application. Flow is currently available as a free beta download for macOS; a Windows version is expected in Q3 2025.
